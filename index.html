What we do

At Gee Emm Aar Chemical, located in the heart of Chennai, Tamil Nadu, we take pride in being a prominent name renowned for our exceptional offerings in the realm of Acids and Industrial Chemicals. With unwavering dedication and a commitment to excellence, we have established ourselves as leading dealers and suppliers in this dynamic industry.

Our Comprehensive Range

We offer a meticulously curated selection of premium-quality Acids and Industrial Chemicals, each chosen to meet the diverse needs of our valued clientele. Our products are not only of the highest quality but also synonymous with durability and long-lasting effectiveness. Whether you require acids for specific industrial applications or chemicals crucial to your processes, we have you covered.

Uncompromised Sourcing

To ensure the utmost satisfaction of our customers, we meticulously source our products from authorized vendors across the country. This meticulous selection process guarantees that our offerings meet the strictest quality standards, providing you with reliable and efficient solutions for your requirements.

Tailored to Perfection

Understanding that each client may have unique needs, we offer the flexibility of customization. Our products can be tailored to align with your precise specifications, ensuring that what you receive is not just a product, but a solution crafted to cater to your individual requirements.

Exceptional Value

We believe that quality should never come at an exorbitant price. That's why we offer our top-notch Acids and Industrial Chemicals at industry-leading prices. This affordability, combined with our uncompromising quality, makes Gee Emm Aar Chemical the destination of choice for businesses seeking exceptional value.

Rigorous Quality Assurance

Quality isn't just a buzzword for us – it's a commitment. Our dedication to providing you with the best is evident from the very beginning. Every product undergoes rigorous quality testing right at the procurement stage. This stringent testing ensures that only products of the highest quality make their way to your doorstep.

At Gee Emm Aar Chemical, our mission is to be more than just suppliers – we aim to be your partners in progress. With a dedication to quality, customization, and customer satisfaction, we stand ready to fulfill your Acids and Industrial Chemical needs.

Experience excellence in every molecule – choose Gee Emm Aar Chemical today.

WHO WE ARE?

Gee Emm Aar Chemical, headquartered in Chennai, Tamil Nadu, stands as a prominent player in the domain of trading premium-quality Acids and Industrial Chemicals. With an unwavering commitment to excellence and customer satisfaction, we have established ourselves as a key dealer and supplier in this competitive industry.

Our Distinct Offerings

Our comprehensive range of Acids and Industrial Chemicals is characterized by its exceptional quality and durability, offering enduring effects for our esteemed clientele. These products are meticulously sourced from authorized vendors across the country, ensuring adherence to stringent quality standards. We take pride in our ability to customize these offerings to precisely match our clients' specifications.

Unparalleled Value Proposition

At Gee Emm Aar Chemical, we place equal emphasis on quality and affordability. Our products are not only known for their superior performance but are also available at industry-leading prices, making us the preferred choice for businesses seeking exceptional value without compromising on excellence.

Guided by Vision

Founded in 1990 under the visionary leadership of "Mr. G. Muthurathinam," our company has achieved remarkable growth in both domestic and international markets. Through his foresight and strategic direction, we have not only developed a substantial customer base but also witnessed remarkable growth in turnover, exceeding millions within a short timeframe.

Timely Delivery and Sustainability

We take pride in delivering our offerings on schedule and at competitive prices. Our services cater to the evolving needs of numerous industrial establishments in various cities. Furthermore, we prioritize our inventory management system and actively consider the environmental impact of our operations.
